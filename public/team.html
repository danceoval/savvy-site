Kevin Fauzie

Savvy AI Co-founder & CEO

Growing up in a rural Indonesian province, my life changed when the internet came to my hometown, giving me access to knowledge and opportunities that I otherwise would not have. Experiencing the democratizing power of tech firsthand inspired me to pursue a career that enables me to do the same for others.

I spent five years at Johnson & Johnson leading strategy & implementation for digital transformation initiatives, building digital health products for emerging markets. I then completed my MBA at Stanford, focusing on strategy and innovation. Most recently, as a product manager at Multiverse, a workforce development unicorn, I managed a portfolio of digital transformation training products that generate $41M in revenue. Democratizing access to strategy & implementation support for digital transformation is a natural next step.
